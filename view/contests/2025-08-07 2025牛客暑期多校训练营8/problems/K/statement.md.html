K. Many Sprinklers!!!

题目描述

There are n n n sprinklers on a two-dimensional Cartesian plane, each of which can water an area inside (boundary included) a circle.

{style="height:auto;width:400px;"}The sprinklers in the sample cases.You need to find the total area that can be watered by the sprinklers.

输入描述

The first line contains an integer T T T ( 1 ≤ T ≤ 10 4 1 \leq T \leq 10^4 1≤T≤104), indicating the number of test cases.Then follow T T T test cases. For each test case:The first line contains an integer n n n ( 1 ≤ n ≤ 10 5 1 \leq n \leq 10^5 1≤n≤105), representing the number of sprinklers.Then n n n lines follow, the i i i-th of which contains three integers x i x_i xi​, y i y_i yi​ and r i r_i ri​ ( − 10 4 ≤ x i , y i ≤ 10 4 -10^4 \leq x_i, y_i \leq 10^4 −104≤xi​,yi​≤104, 1 ≤ r i ≤ 10 4 1 \leq r_i \leq 10^4 1≤ri​≤104), indicating that the i i i-th sprinkler waters an area inside or on the boundary of the circle centered at ( x i , y i ) (x_i, y_i) (xi​,yi​) with radius r i r_i ri​.It is guaranteed that the sum of n n n over all T T T test cases does not exceed 10 5 10^5 105.

输出描述

For each test case, output a real number in one line, representing the total area that can be watered by the sprinklers.Your answer is acceptable if its absolute or relative error does not exceed 10 − 6 10^{-6} 10−6. Formally speaking, suppose that your output is a a a and the jury's answer is b b b, and your output is accepted if and only if ∣ a − b ∣ max ⁡ ( 1 , ∣ b ∣ ) ≤ 10 − 6 \frac{|a - b|}{\max(1, |b|)} \le 10^{-6} max(1,∣b∣)∣a−b∣​≤10−6.
