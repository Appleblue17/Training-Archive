D. Donkey Thinks...

题目描述

Donkey thinks potato chips are the best food ever!

So today, when he decides to go on a long journey, he wants his backpack filled with all kinds of potato chips. He searches through the snack zone in his home and finds lots of potato chips.

To better decide which bags of potato chips to bring with him (a subset of the total bags, possibly none of them), he defines the property of a bag of chips as follows:

  • h i h_i hi​. The happiness this bag of chips can give to Donkey.
  • s i s_i si​. The space this bag of chips occupies.
  • d i d_i di​. The delicacy of this bag of chips.

For simplicity, we note h i , s i , d i h_i,s_i,d_i hi​,si​,di​ as the "happiness", "space" and "delicacy" of the bag. The total occupied space of the chosen bags can't exceed the volume of the backpack, which is V V V.

** **

However, the unoccupied space may cause bumps when Donkey moves during the journey, which further causes value loss. If the chosen chips are i 1 , i 2 , … , i k   ( k ≥ 1 ) i_1,i_2,\dots,i_k\ (k\geq 1) i1​,i2​,…,ik​ (k≥1) and the unoccupied space is U U U, the total value loss on account of bumps is ( d i 1 + d i 2 + ⋯ + d i k ) × U (d_{i_1}+d_{i_2}+\dots+d_{i_k})\times U (di1​​+di2​​+⋯+dik​​)×U. If you choose no bag of chips, the value loss is 0 0 0. Considering both the advantages and disadvantages of bringing chips, the value of the whole backpack is the sum of happiness brought by these bags of chips minus the value loss. Donkey wants to maximize this value, but just can't make the decision. Help is needed for this!

输入描述

Each test contains multiple test cases. The first line contains the number of test cases T   ( 1 ≤ T ≤ 10 4 ) T\ (1≤T≤10^4) T (1≤T≤104) .

Each test case consists of many lines.

The first line contains 2 2 2 integers n , V   ( 1 ≤ n ≤ 10 5 , 1 ≤ V ≤ 500 ) n,V\ (1\leq n\leq 10^5,1\leq V\leq 500) n,V (1≤n≤105,1≤V≤500), the number of chip bags and the total volume of the bag.

Each line from the 2 2 2-nd to the ( n + 1 ) (n+1) (n+1)-th contains 3 3 3 integers h i , s i , d i   ( 1 ≤ s i ≤ 500 , 1 ≤ h i , d i ≤ 10 9 ) h_i,s_i,d_i\ (1\leq s_i\leq 500,1\leq h_i,d_i\leq 10^9) hi​,si​,di​ (1≤si​≤500,1≤hi​,di​≤109), the "happiness", "space" and "delicacy" of the i i i-th bag of chips.

It is guaranteed that ∑ n \sum n ∑n over all test cases in one test will not exceed 10 5 10^5 105, and ∑ V 2 \sum V^2 ∑V2 over all test cases in one test will not exceed 2.5 × 10 5 2.5\times 10^5 2.5×105.

输出描述

For each test case, output one integer: the maximum value.

示例1

输入

2
2 5
10 2 1
2 2 100
2 5
10 2 1
2 3 100

输出

7
12

说明

In the first case, Donkey only chooses the first bag, resulting in a value of 10 − ( 5 − 2 ) × 1 = 7 10-(5-2)\times 1=7 10−(5−2)×1=7.

In the second case, Donkey chooses both the first bag and the second bag, resulting in a value of 10 + 2 − ( 5 − 2 − 3 ) × ( 1 + 101 ) = 12 10+2-(5-2-3)\times(1+101)=12 10+2−(5−2−3)×(1+101)=12.

It can be proved that there are no better strategies.
