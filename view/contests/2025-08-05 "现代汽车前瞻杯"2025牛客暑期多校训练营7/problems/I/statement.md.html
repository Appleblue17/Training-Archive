I. Lava Layer

题目描述

Randomly and uniformly generate an arithmetic expression of length 2 n − 1 \textstyle 2n-1 2n−1. The construction of this expression follows these rules:

  • All characters at odd positions ( 1 \textstyle 1 1-indexed) will be a digit chosen uniformly at random from the given set of digits D \textstyle D D.
  • All characters at even positions ( 1 \textstyle 1 1-indexed) will be an operator chosen uniformly at random from { + , × , ∧ , ∨ , ⊕ } \textstyle \{+, \times, \land, \lor, \oplus\} {+,×,∧,∨,⊕} (addition, multiplication, bitwise AND, bitwise OR, bitwise XOR).
