G. Starlight (starlight)

题目描述

Given a sequence a a a of length n n n and a positive integer m m m, you need to handle q q q operations.Each operation provides x , y , z x, y, z x,y,z, meaning:

  • First, permanently update a x a_x ax​ to y y y.
  • Then, query the minimum number of deletion operations required to ensure that at least z z z elements in the sequence become 0 0 0.

Definition of one deletion operation:

  1. Sort the non-zero elements in ascending order of their values. Let b 1 , b 2 , … , b k b_1, b_2, \ldots, b_k b1​,b2​,…,bk​ be the sequence of their indices after sorting.
  2. Choose an arbitrary non-negative integer k ′ ≤ m k' \leq m k′≤m. For every i i i satisfying 1 ≤ i ≤ min ⁡ ( k , k ′ ) 1 \le i \le\min(k,k') 1≤i≤min(k,k′), set a b i : = a b i − 1 a_{b_i} := a_{b_i} - 1 abi​​:=abi​​−1.

Note:

  • The update to a x a_x ax​ is permanent and affects subsequent operations.
  • The deletion operations are not permanent; the sequence reverts to its current state after each query.
