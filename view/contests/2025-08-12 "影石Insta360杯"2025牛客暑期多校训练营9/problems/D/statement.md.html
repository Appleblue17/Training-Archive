题目描述

Mygo!!!!!, a famous girl band, is going to host a live performance tomorrow! However, they haven't decided their costumes yet, so tonight they will make their costumes out of a piece of fabric. A piece of fabric can be divided into n \textstyle n n parts with equal length, and the color of the i \textstyle i i-th part is a i \textstyle a_i ai​. To simplify input, the fabric will be described as a string consisting only of lowercase letters from a to z, where each letter denotes a specific color.

First, they will use several continuous parts of the fabric to make a costume, which means they will choose l \textstyle l l and r \textstyle r r, where 1 ≤ l ≤ r ≤ n \textstyle 1 \le l \le r \le n 1≤l≤r≤n, and this will make a costume of length r − l + 1 \textstyle r - l + 1 r−l+1, and the color of the i \textstyle i i-th part of the costume, c i \textstyle c_i ci​, will be a l + i − 1 \textstyle a_{l + i - 1} al+i−1​.

In order to make the live even more exciting, they believe that the costume must be beautiful. A beautiful costume c \textstyle c c with length k \textstyle k k is a costume that satisfies c i = c k − i + 1 \textstyle c_i = c_{k - i + 1} ci​=ck−i+1​ for all 1 ≤ i ≤ k \textstyle 1 \le i \le k 1≤i≤k, and it shows the beauty of symmetry.

Now, they choose a beautiful costume with length greater than 1 \textstyle 1 1, and they all go to sleep, except Rana. Rana was very naughty, so she cut Soyorin's costume into two non-empty costumes, which means for a costume c \textstyle c c of length k \textstyle k k, she will choose d \textstyle d d where 1 ≤ d < k \textstyle 1 \le d < k 1≤d<k, and divide the original costume into c [ 1 , d ] \textstyle c[1,d] c[1,d] and c [ d + 1 , k ] \textstyle c[d+1,k] c[d+1,k], then she goes to sleep just like everyone else.

The next morning, Soyorin wakes up and finds her costume cut into two, but there is no time for her to make a new costume, so she has to wear both pieces. However, if both of the two costumes are beautiful, she can just pretend as if nothing happened. Please help Soyorin calculate how many cases exist in which the two costumes are both beautiful. Two cases are considered different if at least one of l \textstyle l l, r \textstyle r r, or d \textstyle d d is different.

输入描述

The first line of input contains a single integer n \textstyle n n ( 1 ≤ n ≤ 3 ⋅ 10 6 ) \textstyle (1 \le n \le 3 \cdot 10^6) (1≤n≤3⋅106) --- the length of the fabric.

The second line contains a string of length n \textstyle n n, consisting only of lowercase letters fromatoz, where the i \textstyle i i-th letter denotes a i \textstyle a_i ai​, the color of the i \textstyle i i-th part of the fabric.

输出描述

Output a single integer, the number of cases in which the two costumes are both beautiful.

示例1

输入

4
aaab

输出

4

示例2

输入

20

输出

备注

For sample one, in the first step there are three possible ways to choose a beautiful costume: aa , aa and aaa , corresponding to l = 1 , r = 2 \textstyle l = 1, r = 2 l=1,r=2, l = 2 , r = 3 \textstyle l = 2, r = 3 l=2,r=3 and l = 1 , r = 3 \textstyle l = 1, r = 3 l=1,r=3. Note that b , corresponding to l = 4 , r = 4 \textstyle l = 4, r = 4 l=4,r=4, is not chosen because the costume length must be greater than 1 \textstyle 1 1.

Now, consider Rana's choice of d \textstyle d d.

  • For aa , there is only one possible d \textstyle d d, namely d = 1 \textstyle d = 1 d=1, which splits it into a and a , both of which are beautiful.
  • For aaa , when d = 1 \textstyle d = 1 d=1, it splits into a and aa , both beautiful; when d = 2 \textstyle d = 2 d=2, it splits into aa and a , both beautiful.

Therefore, there are 2 × 1 + 1 × 2 = 4 \textstyle 2 \times 1 + 1 \times 2 = 4 2×1+1×2=4 cases in total.
