F. Military Training

题目描述

College life has begun! The beginning of college life is military training.

Pog is a pig with two feet. The distance between Pog's two feet is always exactly 1 \textstyle 1 1, and they always stand on two integer grid points on a two-dimensional plane. During military training, the instructor taught Pog a special skill: rotation. The rules are as follows:

  • In each operation, Pog can choose either foot as a pivot, and then rotate the other foot around the pivot by 90 ∘ \textstyle 90^\circ 90∘ (either clockwise or counterclockwise).

Now, Pog is initially standing at two points on the plane, ( s x 1 , s y 1 ) \textstyle (sx_1, sy_1) (sx1​,sy1​) and ( s x 2 , s y 2 ) \textstyle (sx_2, sy_2) (sx2​,sy2​). His goal is to move his two feet onto two other points, ( t x 1 , t y 1 ) \textstyle (tx_1, ty_1) (tx1​,ty1​) and ( t x 2 , t y 2 ) \textstyle (tx_2, ty_2) (tx2​,ty2​), by using the allowed rotations. It does not matter which foot ends up at which target point. The distance between the two feet always remains 1 \textstyle 1 1, no matter how Pog rotates.

Please help Pog determine: what is the minimum number of rotations needed for him to reach the target positions? If it is impossible to reach the target positions, output − 1 \textstyle -1 −1 instead.

输入描述

Each test contains multiple test cases. The first line contains a single integer T   ( 1 ≤ T ≤ 10 5 ) \textstyle T~(1 \le T \le 10^5) T (1≤T≤105) --- the number of test cases.

The only line of each case contains eight integers s x 1 , s y 1 , s x 2 , s y 2 , t x 1 , t y 1 , t x 2 , t y 2   ( ∣ s ∗ ∗ ∣ , ∣ t ∗ ∗ ∣ ≤ 10 9 ) \textstyle sx_1, sy_1, sx_2, sy_2, tx_1, ty_1, tx_2, ty_2 ~ (|s*_*|,|t*_*| \le 10 ^ 9) sx1​,sy1​,sx2​,sy2​,tx1​,ty1​,tx2​,ty2​ (∣s∗∗​∣,∣t∗∗​∣≤109).

It is guaranteed that ∣ s x 1 − s x 2 ∣ + ∣ s y 1 − s y 2 ∣ = ∣ t x 1 − t x 2 ∣ + ∣ t y 1 − t y 2 ∣ = 1 \textstyle |sx_1 - sx_2| + |sy_1 - sy_2| = |tx_1 - tx_2| + |ty_1 - ty_2| = 1 ∣sx1​−sx2​∣+∣sy1​−sy2​∣=∣tx1​−tx2​∣+∣ty1​−ty2​∣=1.

输出描述

For each test case, if Pog can reach the final position, output a single integer --- the minimum number of operations Pog needs to rotate. Otherwise, output − 1 \textstyle -1 −1.
