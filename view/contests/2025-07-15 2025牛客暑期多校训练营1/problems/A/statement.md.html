A. Rectangular Posters

题目描述

A rectangular board with sides parallel to the coordinate axes lies on a two-dimensional plane. The bottom-left corner of the board has coordinates ( 0 , 0 ) (0, 0) (0,0), and the top-right corner has coordinates ( W , H ) (W, H) (W,H).

There are n n n rectangular posters, the i i i-th of which has a width of w i w_i wi​ ( 1 ≤ w i < W 1 \le w_i < W 1≤wi​<W) and a height of h i h_i hi​ ( 1 ≤ h i < H 1 \le h_i < H 1≤hi​<H). These posters are placed fully inside the board randomly and independently, without being rotated or flipped, and the sides are parallel to the coordinate axes. More specifically, the bottom-left corner of the i i i-th poster has coordinates ( x i , y i ) (x_i, y_i) (xi​,yi​), and the top-right corner has coordinates ( x i + w i , y i + h i ) (x_i + w_i, y_i + h_i) (xi​+wi​,yi​+hi​), where each x i x_i xi​ is independently and uniformly randomly chosen from [ 0 , W − w i ] [0, W - w_i] [0,W−wi​], and each y i y_i yi​ is independently and uniformly randomly chosen from [ 0 , H − h i ] [0, H - h_i] [0,H−hi​].

You need to find the expected area covered by the n n n posters modulo 10 9 + 7 10^9+7 109+7.

输入描述

The first line contains three integers n n n ( 1 ≤ n ≤ 120 1 \le n \le 120 1≤n≤120), W W W, and H H H ( 2 ≤ W , H ≤ 10 9 2 \le W, H \le 10^9 2≤W,H≤109), indicating the number of rectangular posters, the width, and the height of the rectangular board.

Then n n n lines follow, the i i i-th of which contains two integers w i w_i wi​ ( 1 ≤ w i < W 1 \le w_i < W 1≤wi​<W) and h i h_i hi​ ( 1 ≤ h i < H 1 \le h_i < H 1≤hi​<H), indicating the width and the height of the i i i-th rectangular poster.

输出描述

Output a line containing an integer, indicating the expected area covered by the n n n posters modulo 10 9 + 7 10^9+7 109+7.

It can be proved that the probability is always a rational number. Additionally, under the constraints of this problem, it can also be proved that when that value is represented as an irreducible fraction p / q p/q p/q, we have q ≢ 0 ( m o d 10 9 + 7 ) q \not\equiv 0 \pmod{10^9+7} q≡0(mod109+7). Thus, there is a unique integer r r r ( 0 ≤ r < 10 9 + 7 0 \le r < 10^9+7 0≤r<109+7) such that p × r ≡ q ( m o d 10 9 + 7 ) p \times r \equiv q \pmod{10^9+7} p×r≡q(mod109+7). This r r r is what we need.

示例1

1 1

输出

1

说明
