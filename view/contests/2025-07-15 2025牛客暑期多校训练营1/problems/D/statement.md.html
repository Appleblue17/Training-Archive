D. LCM Challenge

题目描述

There are lots of things to do in this contest besides this problem, so let's make it quick.

Given two integers n n n and p p p, where p p p is prime, you need to find the value of LCM ( 1 , 2 , 3 , … , n )   m o d   p \text{LCM}(1,2,3,\ldots,n) \bmod p LCM(1,2,3,…,n)modp, i.e., the least common multiple of 1 , 2 , 3 , … , n 1,2,3,\ldots,n 1,2,3,…,n modulo p p p.

输入描述

The only line contains two integers n n n and p p p ( 1 ≤ n < p < 10 10 1 \le n < p < 10^{10} 1≤n<p<1010), where p p p is prime.

输出描述

Output a line containing a single integer, indicating the value of LCM ( 1 , 2 , 3 , … , n )   m o d   p \text{LCM}(1,2,3,\ldots,n) \bmod p LCM(1,2,3,…,n)modp.

示例1

输入

10 9999999967

输出

2520

说明

For the first sample case, LCM ( 1 , 2 , 3 , … , 10 ) = 2 3 × 3 2 × 5 × 7 = 2520 \text{LCM}(1,2,3,\ldots,10) = 2^3 \times 3^2 \times 5 \times 7 = 2520 LCM(1,2,3,…,10)=23×32×5×7=2520.

示例2

输入

30 9999999967

输出

9089570456

说明

For the second sample case, LCM ( 1 , 2 , 3 , … , 30 ) = 2   329   089   562   800 \text{LCM}(1,2,3,\ldots,30) = 2\,329\,089\,562\,800 LCM(1,2,3,…,30)=2329089562800.

示例3

输入

9999999966 9999999967

输出

6047288450
